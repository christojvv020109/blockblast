How to Play

  • 🧩Drag blocks onto the board.
  • ➖Fill a full row or column to clear it.
  • 🎁Clear 5 lines to finish a level and win 5 prizes.
  • 🔒Prizes stay wrapped — you'll only see them once you've collected all 5!
  • ⬆️Your prizes then help you in the next level.
  • 🗑️No room? Drag a block to the trash to throw it away.
